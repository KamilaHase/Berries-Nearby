Pick Home-Grown Fruits

Sign up as a member and get access to our lists of farmers who offer their fruits for self-picking in the Gothenburg area. You will be able to pick fruits and vegetables that grew in one of the local gardens, are of organic quality and free or just a little charge.

Offer Your Fruits To Others

Do you want to offer your garden fruits to others? Were your trees generous and there is too many fruits for your own consumption? Is your garden located in Gothenburg area? Become our member and share the information with the closed group. You will be able to share as many/little details as you wish and offer other members to come and pick themselves or you may sell your own already picked fruits.
